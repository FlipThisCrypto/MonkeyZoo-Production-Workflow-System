# July 2026 idea (rough)

Title: The Broken Signal

Rough idea:
The MonkeyZoo squad finds an old FusionZoo signal still broadcasting from a forgotten part of Zoo City. It should have shut down years ago, but someone has kept it alive.

NeonBlue believes it is a rescue call. Emo thinks it is a trap. Zombie Monkey recognizes the signal but refuses to explain how he knows it.

The squad follows the signal into an abandoned FusionZoo relay room and finds evidence that some monkeys were left behind during the early fusion experiments. These monkeys are not fully saved, not fully lost, and not sure they want anyone interfering.

The emotional center should be about guilt, survival, and what it means to walk away from people who were not ready to leave.

End the issue with a teaser that the signal was not calling for help. It was calling something back.
