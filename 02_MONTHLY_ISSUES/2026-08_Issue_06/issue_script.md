# MZ-2026-08-06 — The Broken Signal — Script

## Cover surfaces

**Front outside cover:** The Old Quarter at night. The squad small on a
rooftop edge, silhouetted against a huge, ancient relay mast whose single red
light blinks — the light source of the whole cover. In the light's cone, faint
and almost missable: half-rendered monkey outlines, unfinished, translucent.
Patch stands one step ahead of the squad, the only one facing the mast
directly. Title: THE BROKEN SIGNAL.

**Front inside cover (blurb):**
Every network gets shut down eventually.
The servers come down. The signs come down. The people who ran it move on.
Somewhere in the Old Quarter, one signal didn't get the memo.
It's been repeating the same message for thirteen years.
Tonight, somebody finally answers.

**Rear inside cover (stinger):**
They promised to come back.
They meant it.
So did the signal.

**Rear outside cover (teaser):** Black field. The wrong-rhythm red blink,
bottom-left. Far upper-right, beyond a horizon line: a second red light,
blinking in answer. Copy: "NEXT: IT HEARD. — MonkeyZoo."
