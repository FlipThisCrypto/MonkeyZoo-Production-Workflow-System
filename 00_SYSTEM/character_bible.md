**Version:** 1.1 · **Last updated:** 2026-07-06

v1.1 replaces v1.0's `[PROPOSED]` visual specs with designs extracted from the
creator's reference images (`Bible and other files/{Ash,NeonBlue,scarline,Static}/`,

Visual specs below marked **LOCKED (refs)** are confirmed against those images.
Personalities were already locked from published Editions 1–5 and are unchanged.

## Shared MonkeyZoo Anatomy Template (all leads — LOCKED refs)

- Chibi proportions: head ≈ 55–60% of total height, oversized round head.
- Huge white oval eyes with round black dot pupils; tiny dark kidney-bean nose
  (or two nostril dots) set high; simple wide mouths.
- **The Emo Faction uniform (all six leads wear variations of it):** pale
  ash-white/grey face and chest vs brown ears/arms/lower body/tail (two-tone
  split); black punk pants with rows of silver or white round studs; black
  studded wrist cuffs; chunky grey-soled boots/creepers/sneakers with tread
  stripes; grey under-eye rings (the faction's tired look).
- 3–4 short stitch/hash marks on the chest (vertical sternum seam or
  horizontal hatch lines) — the living-toy motif.
- Faint darker tiger-stripe markings on forearms (recurring, optional per panel).
- Mitten hands (four-fingered mitten-like paws — never articulated fingers),
  long curled monkey tail.
- Thick uniform black outlines. **Flat colors, NO gradients** — the only
  sanctioned soft shading: grey eye-bags and NeonBlue's cyan hair-root glow.
- **Leads are differentiated primarily by HAIR.** In silhouette/back shots the
  hair does the identification work.

## Moodz

- **Character Name:** Moodz
- **Canon ID:** MZ-CHAR-001
- **Role:** Protagonist; the emotional center of the book
- **Core personality (LOCKED, Ed.1–6):** Emotionally expressive but guarded.
  Skeptical of systems, observant, slow to trust, fierce when a line is
  crossed. Feels everything, admits little.
- **Reference basis:** Emo ref #973; root webp #1136; Fusion Squad PDF "goth
  monkey" (pale face, black emo hair w/ blue accents, studded leather,
  deadpan skeptic — personality match is near-exact)
- **Visual silhouette:** Heavy black emo fringe breaking the round head outline
- **Head/hair (LOCKED refs):** Black emo fringe sweeping across one eye with a
  BLUE streak/accent; refs alternate between blue streak, blue headband, and
  blue beanie — streak is default, headband/beanie are allowed variations
- **Eyes:** Huge white ovals, dot pupils, grey eye-shadow rings; dark
  panda-like eye patches in some refs (allowed)
- **Fur/skin:** Brown fur; pale grey-white face and stitched white chest
- **Clothing (LOCKED refs):** Open black studded leather jacket over white
  shirt/chest; black studded pants; black studded wrist cuffs; grey platform
  boots with tread stripes
- **Signature colors:** Black / pale white / blue accent
- **Signature prop:** None. Moodz carries nothing; that's the point.
- **Default emotional state:** Wary, low-key melancholic, deadpan
- **Comedy style:** Deadpan understatement ("Yeah. We're breaking stuff. Cool
  I guess." — PDF goth monkey, same voice)
- **Speech pattern:** Short declaratives. Rarely more than one sentence.
- **Forbidden changes:** No smile-by-default; fringe never removed; blue
  accent never recolored; no new scars/tattoos; no visible fingers
- **Allowed variations:** Blue streak ↔ blue headband ↔ blue beanie; rain-wet
  fringe; eye-shadow intensity
- **Common poses:** Arms crossed; looking back over shoulder; studying his
  reflection; standing apart
- **Canon history:** Ed.4 fusion abort ("STOP IT" → "Maybe later"); Issue 05
  calm abort ("Not today"); Issue 06 asks the Pending what they want
- **Prompt token:** `mz_moodz`
- **Prompt descriptor:** pale-faced brown monkey, black emo fringe with blue
  streak covering one eye, grey eye rings, open black studded jacket over
  stitched white chest, black studded pants and cuffs, grey platform boots
- **LoRA name:** `MZ_Moodz_v1.safetensors` (train at 20+ approved refs)
- **Reference image folder:** `03_APPROVED_CANON/approved_characters/moodz/`
  (webp #1136 staged as candidate; needs more refs)
- **QA checklist:** Black fringe + blue accent · pale face · grey eye rings ·
  studded jacket/pants/cuffs · tread boots · stitch marks · no fingers

## NeonBlue

- **Eyes (LOCKED refs):** Huge round whites, dot pupils, heavy gunmetal-grey
  under-eye bags in every image; lids often half-lowered
- **Fur/skin:** Chocolate-brown fur; pale ash-GREY face and belly patch;
  espresso tiger-stripes on forearms (canon)
- **Clothing (LOCKED refs):** Black vest/tank framing the pale belly (3–4
  chest hash marks); black punk pants with WHITE stud rows; black studded
  cuffs with white studs; grey-and-black skate sneakers with pale treaded soles
- **Signature colors:** White + electric cyan (hair) / chocolate brown /

- **Signature prop:** Silver gauge/plug earring in LEFT ear (near-every image)
- **Default emotional state:** Effortfully up — deadpan face, hopeful engine

- **Speech pattern:** Slogan-shaped sentences; ad-copy cadence when insecure
