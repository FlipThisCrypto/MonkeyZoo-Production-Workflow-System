# MonkeyZoo Canon Conflicts and Uncertainties

## Visual Canon
- Glasses rule verified against approved reference sheets: Clever is the only character with clear round black-rimmed glasses.
- Static has heavy grey under-eye rings that can read like goggles at small sizes; existing bible says these are eye rings and forbids headphones.
- NeonBlue `neonblue_18_sleepy.png`, Moodz sleepy/defeated poses, and TwoTone sleepy/defeated poses can read as dark shades in thumbnails; treat as closed eyelids/eye bags unless creator says otherwise.
- Super wears a red domino mask. This is not glasses.
- Cheeky has huge blue eyes. These are not glasses/goggles in the current approved refs.
- Scarline: existing bible says the red line is a hair streak, not a facial scar; no facial scar seen in approved refs.
- Super cape appears in older/PDF archetype and loose art, but the approved generated pose set emphasizes suit/mask and does not consistently show a cape. Mark cape as variant until clarified.
- Emo generic approved refs are visually close to Moodz. Current system bible treats Emo as generic faction, while July draft lists Emo separately from Moodz. Needs creator decision.

## Naming / Canon Status
- Clever, Cheeky, Lil Devil, Super Monkey, Zombie, and Emo may be faction/series labels as much as personal names. They have approved refs, but personal-name status is not fully documented.
- Patch is documented in continuity but no individual reference image was located.
- The Keeper is documented as unseen; no image or personal traits located.
- The Stayed and The Pending are group labels/lore populations, not inventoried as individual personal names in this pass.
